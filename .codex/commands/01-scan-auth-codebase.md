# Command 01: Scan Auth Codebase

## Goal

Understand SurveyFlow structure before editing.

## Steps

1. Open `package.json`.
2. Confirm TanStack Router, React, lucide-react, framer-motion, Tailwind, and shadcn dependencies.
3. Open `components.json` and confirm aliases.
4. Open `tsconfig.json` and confirm `@/*`.
5. Inspect `src/routes`.
6. Inspect existing auth links in `src/features/data.ts` and `src/components/shared/Navbar.tsx`.
7. Inspect `src/components/ui`.
8. Identify missing shadcn components from `button input label checkbox card separator`.
9. Summarize chosen route, missing components, and files to edit before changing code.

## Do Not

Do not edit files during this command.
